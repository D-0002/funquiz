How to Play How to Play
START THE GAME
Launch the app and choose STUDENT or TEACHER profile. Log in or sign up if you're a new user.
PLAY MENU
Tap "PLAY" on the main menu. Select a difficulty level: EASY MEDIUM HARD EXTREME
ANSWER QUESTIONS
A question will be shown at the top (e.g., a definition). Use the letter grid to tap and form the correct word. The blanks above the grid show how many letters the answer has. Use the backspace button to remove letters if you make a mistake.
SUBMIT & PROGRESS
Tap the arrow button to submit your answer. Correct answers let you move to the next question. Each level has 5 questions. Finish them to unlock the next difficulty.
OTHER FEATURES
Tap PROFILE to view your progress and details. In SETTINGS, toggle BGM, sound effects, and vibration on/off. Check the LEADERBOARD to see your ranking compared to others.
